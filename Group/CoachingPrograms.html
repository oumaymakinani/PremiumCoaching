Meet the Coaches

Michael Carter

Specialist in Tactical Play & Fitness.

He has over 10 years of experience and trains players in tactical strategies and game awareness. His sessions cover defensive structures and endurance-building drills to enhance performance throughout the game. This training session is ideal for those looking to improve their decision-making, positioning, and stamina.

Johnathan Booker

Expert in Speed & Agility Training.

Johnathan Booker focuses on speed, acceleration, and agility drills to enhance reaction time and footwork. His sessions use ladder drills and resistance training to help players outpace defenders, recover faster, and make explosive plays.

Samantha Radcliff

Advanced Ball Control & Dribbling Coach.

Samantha is known for her exceptional ball mastery techniques, teaching players to maintain control under pressure, execute quick directional changes, and take on defenders. Her coaching focuses on shielding, feints, and close ball control to create opportunities to attack.
